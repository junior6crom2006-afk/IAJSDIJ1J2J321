Other Ways to Give

  • Donating stock directly to a charity is a tax-savvy way to give. Donate your appreciated stock to increase your impact and support organic futures. Contact Willow Aray at waray@ccof.org to begin your stock donation process.

  • Our dream is to make organic the norm–no matter how long it takes. You can support the ongoing efforts of the CCOF Foundation and the future of organic by making a planned gift.

    Leaving a bequest to the CCOF Foundation is a thoughtful and easy way to provide for the future of the organic community.

    Learn more and see sample templates

  • A DAF is like an investment account that supports charitable organizations you care about. When you contribute cash, securities, or other assets to a donor-advised fund at a public charity, you are often eligible for an immediate tax deduction. Those funds can then be invested for tax-free growth. Gifts can be recommended to any eligible IRS-qualified public charity.

    Tell your financial advisor you’d like to support the CCOF Foundation, EIN 30-0106255.

  • Offer your customers a way to contribute to the organic community by donating a portion of your organic product sales to the CCOF Foundation. Let us help you design a program to fit the needs of your company—contact us to learn more.

  • Offer your customers the opportunity to contribute to the organic community by running donation campaigns at checkout, either online or at in-person brick and mortal stores. The CCOF Foundation will provide photo, video, copy, and in-store collateral and training materials for your staff to make the organic message a success. Contact us to learn more.
